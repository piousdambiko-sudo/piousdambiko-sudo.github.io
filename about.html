About Poultry Paradise

Learn more about our story and commitment to quality

Our Story

Founded in 2014, Poultry Paradise has grown from a small family farm to one of Zimbabwe's leading suppliers of premium quality chickens. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide healthy, ethically raised chickens to our local community.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art poultry farm spanning over 50 acres, utilizing modern farming techniques while maintaining our commitment to animal welfare and sustainable practices. We supply to individuals, restaurants, hotels, and retail chains across the country.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality poultry products while maintaining ethical farming practices, ensuring animal welfare, and delivering exceptional customer service. We strive to be the most trusted name in poultry supply across Zimbabwe.

Our Vision

To become the leading poultry supplier in Southern Africa, known for innovation, sustainability, and unwavering commitment to quality. We envision a future where healthy, locally-sourced poultry is accessible to all communities.
